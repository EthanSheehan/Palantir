    --- New Mode Physics ---
    - TestSupportMode: UAV in SUPPORT mode with tracked_target_id orbits at ~3km (SUPPORT_ORBIT_RADIUS_DEG=0.027). After N ticks, distance to target stays within 0.8x-1.2x of orbit radius. Uses _turn_toward() for smooth arcs (same pattern as FOLLOW).
    - TestVerifyMode: UAV in VERIFY mode with tracked_target_id and sensors=["EO_IR"] flies perpendicular cross pattern. UAV in VERIFY with sensors=["SAR"] flies parallel track.     - TestOverwatchMode: UAV in OVERWATCH mode generates 2-waypoint racetrack (stored in u.overwatch_waypoints). Waypoints are clamped to theater bounds. UAV alternates between waypoints. After many ticks, UAV has visited both waypoints.
    - TestBdaMode: UAV in BDA mode orbits tightly at BDA_ORBIT_RADIUS_DEG=0.009. bda_timer starts at BDA_DURATION_SEC=30.0 and decrements per tick. When bda_timer <= 0, mode transitions to SEARCH and tracked_target_id is cleared.
    - TestModeExclusion: UAVs in SUPPORT/VERIFY/OVERWATCH/BDA are NOT processed by UAV.update() in tick() (prevented by exclusion tuple at line 550). Verify no double-update by checking position moves only once per tick.

    --- Autonomy System ---
    - TestAutonomyManual: With autonomy_level="MANUAL", _evaluate_autonomy() does NOT change any UAV mode, even when trigger conditions exist.
    - TestAutonomyAutonomous: With autonomy_level="AUTONOMOUS", when trigger conditions match AUTONOMOUS_TRANSITIONS table, UAV mode changes immediately (no pending transition).
    - TestAutonomySupervised: With autonomy_level="SUPERVISED", trigger creates a pending_transitions entry with mode, reason, expires_at. Before expiry, mode is unchanged. After expires_at, mode auto-applies and pending entry is removed.
    - TestPerDroneOverride: UAV with autonomy_override="MANUAL" stays manual even when fleet is AUTONOMOUS. UAV with autonomy_override="AUTONOMOUS" transitions even when fleet is MANUAL.
    - TestApproveTransition: Calling sim.approve_transition(uav_id) on a pending transition applies the mode immediately and removes the pending entry.
    - TestRejectTransition: Calling sim.reject_transition(uav_id) removes the pending entry without changing mode.
